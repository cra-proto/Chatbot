CRA Chatbot

The Canada Revenue Agency’s (CRA) Chatbot available 24/7 throughout the year to help answer frequently asked tax and benefits questions. The chatbot service is striving to get you the information you need in the CRA pages on Canada.ca

Follow the steps below to ask questions to the CRA chatbot.

  1. Start a conversation

    Click on the icon Chat with Charlie in the lower right corner of this page, to open the chatbot widget.

    • Pages where you can find the CRA chatbot

      You can open the CRA chatbot widget from the following pages on Canada.ca:

    • Canada Revenue Agency
    • Personal income tax
    • Get ready to do your taxes
    • Contact the Canada Revenue Agency
    • [add missing pages]
    • Privacy disclaimer

      Do not include any personal information. Your questions may be stored and analyzed to improve the chatbot interactivity. However, the CRA will not store or use your profile information, past history, or other interactions. For more information about privacy, refer to the Privacy statement.

    • Technical difficulties when using the chatbot

      If you are having technical difficulties accessing the CRA chatbot widget, check if your browser is up to date or try again using another browser. The current version of the following browser are compatible with the CRA chatbot:

    • Chrome
    • Edge
    • Safari

  2. Ask your question

    Type you question in the message box, and click the icon or press Enter to send.

    The best way for the CRA chatbot to understand you is to:

    • Ask one question at a time
    • Write in complete sentences
    • not use any personal information or complexe situation

      • You can type “start over” at any point during to reset the conversation.

    • Example of questions
    • How do I register for My Business Account?
    • How can I change my banking information?
    • Where can I change my address?
    • How do I change my marital status with CRA?
    • How do I check my CRA mail online?
    • How do I make a payment?
    • How to complete and file a return?
    • Where do I go for free tax filing?
    • How do I change my return?
    • Which tax deductions, credits, or expenses, I can claim ?
    • Who can claim the disability tax credit?
    • How do I get a business number?
    • How to open a GST/HST account?
    • What is the child and family benefits calculator?
    • Where do I learn about personal income taxes?
    • How do I know if the CRA is contacting me?
      •
    • What to do if don't get an answer

      If the chatbot can’t answer, try the following:

    • ask your question differently
    • use one keyword, for example: T2, T400A, charities or tuition.
    • Reset the conversation by typing “start over”

      • If you are still unable to get the information you need, you may find your answer in your CRA sign-in services or go to Contact the Canada Revenue Agency.

  3. Give your feedback

    You can find the link to send feedback in the the CRA Chatbot introcudtion message or included in certain answer, or simply type "feedback" to get the link to the short survey.

    By submitting feedback about your experience with the CRA chatbot, you can help us improve our services.

    Your feedback may be about any of the following:

    • Poor or misleading information
    • Ease or difficulty getting answers
    • Mistakes that cause a misunderstanding
    • Suggestions to improve service
    • Compliments about good service
    • Other chatbot-related experiences

    For feedback about accessibility: Send accessibility feedback
