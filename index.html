CRA chatbot

The Canada Revenue Agency’s (CRA) chatbot is available 24/7 throughout the year to help answer frequently asked questions. The chatbot service is striving to get you the information you need in the CRA pages on Canada.ca

Follow the steps below to ask questions to the CRA chatbot.

  1. Open the chatbot

    Select the icon Chat with Charlie to open the chat widget. You will find the widget in the lower right corner on the CRA homepage and other CRA pages on Canada.ca.

    • Privacy disclaimer

      Do not include any personal information. Your questions may be stored and analyzed to improve the chatbot interactivity. However, the CRA will not store or use your profile information, history, or other interactions. For more information about privacy, refer to the Privacy statement.

    • Issues opening the chatbot

      If you are having technical difficulties accessing the CRA chatbot widget, check if your browser is up to date or try again using another browser. The current version of the following browsers are compatible with the CRA chatbot:

    • Chrome
    • Edge
    • Safari

  2. Ask your question

    Type you question in the message box, and select the icon or Enter to send.

    The best way for the CRA chatbot to help you resolve your issue is to:

    • Ask one question at a time
    • Write complete sentences
    • Describe a simple situation without personal or ambiguous information
    • What to do if don't get an answer

      If the chatbot can’t answer, try the following:

    • Ask your question differently
    • Use one keyword, for example T2, T400A, charities or tuition.
    • Reset the conversation by typing “start over”


      • If you are still unable to get the information you need, you may find your answer in your CRA sign-in services or go to Contact the Canada Revenue Agency.

  3. Give your feedback

    You can find the link to send feedback in the CRA chatbot introduction message, or type "feedback" to get the link to the short survey.

    By submitting feedback about your experience with the CRA chatbot, you can help us improve our services.

    Your feedback may be about any of the following:

    • Compliments about good service
    • Suggestions to improve service
    • Ease or difficulty getting answers
    • Poor or misleading information
    • Mistakes that cause a misunderstanding
    • Other chatbot-related experience
